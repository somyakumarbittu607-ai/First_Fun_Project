Pokémon is a world filled with amazing creatures that trainers catch and train to battle each other. Each Pokémon has unique powers, types, and abilities. The journey teaches friendship, teamwork, and courage.

Ash Ketchum’s journey began in Pallet Town with his partner Pikachu. Through his travels, Ash met friends, faced challenges, and grew stronger with every battle. His dream was to become a Pokémon Master.
